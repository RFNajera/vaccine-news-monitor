At a glance

Why vaccines matter

  • 4–5 million deaths a year prevented globally by vaccines
  • 154 million lives saved over 50 years of the EPI program
  • 2 doses of MMR are about 97% effective against measles
  • Vaccines are among the most cost-effective public health tools ever developed

Method

How this works

This page reads a scheduled JSON feed generated from news search results. Headlines are deduplicated, checked for recency, classified by vaccine and article type, and grouped by source quality.

It is meant to be a monitoring tool, not a formal systematic review. Useful for signal, not the final word.
